# ⚡ About NSPCL Power Quiz ## SSC C&M Function – NSPCL Power-Up Quiz **NSPCL Power Quiz** is an interactive knowledge platform designed to enhance awareness and learning about the **SSC C&M (Contracts & Materials) Function** of **NTPC-SAIL Power Company Limited (NSPCL)**. The quiz aims to promote continuous learning among employees by providing an engaging way to understand important concepts related to: ✅ Contracts Management ✅ Procurement Processes ✅ Materials Management ✅ Vendor Management ✅ Tendering Procedures ✅ Supply Chain Practices ✅ Commercial & C&M Guidelines ## 🎯 Objective of the Quiz The main objective of the NSPCL Power Quiz is to: * Improve functional knowledge of SSC C&M activities. * Encourage employees to learn through an interactive quiz format. * Create awareness about procurement and contract management practices. * Support knowledge sharing and continuous improvement culture. ## 🏢 About NSPCL **NTPC-SAIL Power Company Limited (NSPCL)** is a joint venture company of **NTPC Limited and Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL)** with equal 50:50 shareholding. NSPCL operates captive power plants to meet the power requirements of SAIL steel plants at locations including Bhilai, Durgapur and Rourkela. NSPCL focuses on safe, reliable and sustainable power generation while delivering value through operational excellence, innovation and teamwork. ## 📚 About SSC C&M Function The **Contracts & Materials (C&M) Function** plays a key role in ensuring efficient procurement of materials, services and contracts required for smooth operation of NSPCL plants. Major activities include: 🔹 Procurement planning 🔹 Tender processing 🔹 Contract finalization 🔹 Supplier and vendor management 🔹 Material availability management 🔹 Coordination with user departments, finance and stores ## 🏆 Learn • Compete • Improve The NSPCL Power Quiz provides participants an opportunity to: ⭐ Test their C&M knowledge ⭐ Learn important processes ⭐ Improve functional understanding ⭐ Participate in a knowledge-building initiative **“Knowledge is Power – Keep Learning, Keep Growing.”** ⚡ **NSPCL Power Quiz – Empowering Knowledge Through Learning**
