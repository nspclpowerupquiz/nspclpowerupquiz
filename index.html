About NSPCL

NTPC-SAIL Power Company Limited (NSPCL) is a joint venture between NTPC Limited and Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL).

NSPCL operates captive power plants and contributes towards reliable and efficient power generation for industrial growth.

With focus on safety, sustainability, operational excellence and innovation, NSPCL continues to strengthen India's power sector.
