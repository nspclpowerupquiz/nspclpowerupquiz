About NSPCL Power-Up Quiz

Empowering employees through continuous learning, technical excellence, and healthy competition.

Who We Are

The NSPCL Power-Up Quiz is a digital knowledge platform designed to strengthen employee understanding of the SSC Contracts & Materials (C&M) function. It encourages continuous learning through engaging quizzes, performance tracking, and recognition of achievements.

Our Objectives

📘 Learn

Build strong knowledge of procurement, contracts, materials management, inventory control, and supply chain processes.

🏆 Compete

Participate in quizzes, improve your ranking, earn badges, and compete with colleagues.

📈 Improve

Track learning progress through analytics and identify areas for continuous improvement.

Topics Covered

📄 Procurement

Purchase procedures, tendering, bidding, and procurement policies.

🤝 Contracts

Contract administration, risk management, claims, and compliance.

📦 Materials

Inventory management, warehousing, codification, and stock control.

🚚 Supply Chain

Vendor management, logistics, transportation, and supply chain best practices.

⚖ Policies

NTPC/NSPCL guidelines, ethics, transparency, and procurement governance.

💻 Digital Systems

SAP, e-Procurement, GeM, and digital business processes.

Why Participate?

✔ Improve technical knowledge
✔ Prepare for departmental assessments
✔ Strengthen procurement and contract management skills
✔ Learn through interactive quizzes and daily challenges
✔ Earn certificates and recognition for your achievements
