About NSPCL Power Quiz

NSPCL Power Quiz is an interactive learning platform created to improve knowledge about SSC C&M (Contracts & Materials) functions.

This quiz covers procurement, contracts management, materials management, vendor management and supply chain practices.

Objective

  • Improve C&M functional knowledge
  • Encourage continuous learning
  • Promote knowledge sharing
  • Understand procurement processes
