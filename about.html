Who We Are

The NSPCL Power-Up Quiz is an interactive digital learning platform developed to strengthen the technical knowledge of employees working in the SSC Contracts & Materials (C&M) function. The platform provides engaging quizzes, performance tracking, leaderboards, analytics and recognition to encourage continuous learning and professional excellence.

🚀 Platform Highlights

📚 Learn

Master procurement, contracts, inventory management, supply chain and power sector concepts.

⚡ Practice

Attempt unlimited quizzes with instant feedback and improve your technical knowledge.

🏆 Compete

Participate in quizzes, climb the leaderboard, earn certificates and recognition.

📊 Analytics

Track your learning progress, monitor scores and identify improvement areas.
